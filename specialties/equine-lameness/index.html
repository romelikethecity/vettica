About equine lameness veterinary data

Gait analysis, hoof diagnostics, and musculoskeletal treatment. Vettica provides verified contact information for DVMs specializing in this area, sourced from state veterinary licensing boards and cross-referenced against professional directories.

Each record includes the veterinarian's name, verified email, direct phone number, practice name and address, license number with active status, and specialty classification. All emails are deliverability-tested. All phone numbers are verified against active carrier records.

Frequently Asked Questions

What equine lameness data do you have?

Each record includes the DVM's full name, verified email, direct phone number, practice name, practice address, license number, license status, and specialty type. Custom fields are available on request.

How do you verify the data?

We source raw data from state veterinary licensing boards, then cross-reference against AVMA directories, DEA registrations, and business listings. Emails are deliverability-tested. Phone numbers are verified against active carrier records. The full process takes 3-5 business days per list.

Do you cover equine lameness vets nationwide?

We cover every US state. Each state's veterinary licensing board publishes DVM records on its own schedule. We maintain pipelines for all 50 states and adjust our sourcing to match each board's data refresh cycle.
