Frequently Asked Questions

What veterinary specialties does Vettica cover?

We cover 8 major categories: small animal, large animal, equine, exotic and avian, emergency and critical care, specialty referral, mobile and rural, and mixed practice. Within these categories, we track over 30 subspecialties.

Can I filter by multiple specialties?

Yes. You can request a list that combines multiple specialties (for example, small animal + emergency practices in a given state). Custom combinations are standard on every order.

How do you determine a practice's specialty?

We combine state board licensing data, practice self-reported information, AVMA classification, and verified practice focus from business listings. Practices with multiple focus areas are tagged with all applicable specialties.
