Frequently Asked Questions

What data does Vettica provide for practice management software?

Standard fields: DVM name, email (deliverability-tested), direct phone, practice name and address, state license number, active/inactive status, and specialty classification. We can add technology detection or firmographic data for an additional fee.

How is the data verified?

Every contact starts with a state board license record. We verify the DVM is actively licensed, confirm their practice affiliation, validate their email address, and check their phone number. Records that fail any verification step are flagged or excluded.

How fresh is the data?

Lists are built fresh for each order. We don't sell pre-built databases. When you place an order, we pull the latest licensing data and run verification at that point. Typical data age at delivery is under 30 days for license status and under 7 days for contact verification.

What specialties can I filter by?

We segment by 8 major categories (small animal, large animal, equine, exotic, emergency, specialty referral, mobile, and mixed practice) and over 30 subtypes within those categories. Subtypes include specific focuses like cardiology, oncology, bovine, equine sports medicine, and more.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Complex builds with custom criteria may take 5-7 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive campaigns.
