Veterinary Data for Recruiting Agencies

Source DVM candidates and place them faster. Built for companies like DVM staffing agencies, locum tenens firms, vet school career offices.

The problem

The veterinary industry faces a well-documented workforce shortage. Placing DVMs means knowing which practices are hiring, what specialties they need, and how to reach the decision-maker. LinkedIn profiles for veterinarians are often incomplete or outdated.

How Vettica solves this

Vettica provides verified DVM contact data sourced from state licensing boards. You get active license status, practice affiliation, specialty focus, and direct contact information. Build candidate pipelines by specialty and geography. Reach hiring managers at practices across all 50 states.

Frequently Asked Questions

What data does Vettica provide for veterinary recruiting?

Each record includes the DVM's full name, verified email, direct phone number, practice name, practice address, license number, license status, and specialty type. Custom fields are available on request.

How is the data verified?

We source raw data from state veterinary licensing boards, then cross-reference against AVMA directories, DEA registrations, and business listings. Emails are deliverability-tested. Phone numbers are verified against active carrier records. The full process takes 3-5 business days per list.

How fresh is the data?

Each list is built to order using the most current state board records available at the time. We don't recycle old data. Email and phone verification happens in the final 48 hours before delivery, so contact accuracy reflects current status.

What specialties can I filter by?

You can filter by 8 hub categories and 30+ subspecialties. Want only equine sports medicine vets in Kentucky? Or exotic avian specialists in California? We can build that list. The segmentation comes from license specialization data and practice focus analysis.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Complex builds with custom criteria may take 5-7 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive campaigns.
