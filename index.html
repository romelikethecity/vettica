Frequently Asked Questions

What data fields are included?

Each record includes the DVM's full name, verified email, direct phone number, practice name, practice address, license number, license status, and specialty type. Custom fields are available on request.

How is the data verified?

We source raw data from state veterinary licensing boards, then cross-reference against AVMA directories, DEA registrations, and business listings. Emails are deliverability-tested. Phone numbers are verified against active carrier records. The full process takes 3-5 business days per list.

How fresh is the data?

Lists are built fresh for each order. We don't sell pre-built databases. When you place an order, we pull the latest licensing data and run verification at that point. Typical data age at delivery is under 30 days for license status and under 7 days for contact verification.

Do you cover all 50 states?

Yes. We cover all 50 states plus DC. Coverage is strongest in states with publicly accessible electronic licensing databases, which is most of them. A handful of states require additional sourcing methods, but we've built reliable pipelines for every state.

What specialties can you segment by?

We segment by 8 major categories (small animal, large animal, equine, exotic, emergency, specialty referral, mobile, and mixed practice) and over 30 subtypes within those categories. Subtypes include specific focuses like cardiology, oncology, bovine, equine sports medicine, and more.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Complex builds with custom criteria or very large geographies may take 5-7 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive projects.

Is there a minimum order?

No minimum. You can order a list for a single ZIP code or an entire state. Pricing is per-project, based on the size and complexity of your request.
