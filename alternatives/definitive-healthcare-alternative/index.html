Definitive Healthcare Alternative for Vet Practices

Definitive Healthcare and veterinary

Definitive Healthcare is the gold standard for hospital and health system data. If you're selling to hospitals, IDNs, or physician groups, it's excellent. But Definitive's coverage of veterinary practices is limited because their data model is built around human healthcare.

Veterinary licensing is administered by different state boards than medical licensing. The specialty taxonomy is different. The practice structure is different. Definitive wasn't built to handle these differences.

Where Vettica fills the gap

Vettica is built specifically for veterinary. We source from state veterinary licensing boards (not medical boards). We classify practices using veterinary specialty taxonomy (not human healthcare taxonomy). We verify contacts against veterinary-specific directories.

If you're already using Definitive for your hospital and physician data, Vettica is the complementary product for your veterinary market. Same quality standards, different data domain.

Pricing comparison

Definitive Healthcare pricing is enterprise-level, typically $50K-$100K+ per year depending on modules. For teams that only need veterinary data, this is massive overkill.

Vettica charges $0.50-$1.50 per record, per project. No annual contract. Start with 20 free records to evaluate quality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vettica a full replacement for Definitive Healthcare?

Vettica replaces the veterinary-specific data you'd get from a generic tool. It doesn't replace platform features like intent data, workflow automation, or hospital system mapping. Many teams use Vettica for vet contacts alongside their existing tools.

Can I try before committing?

Yes. We offer 20 free verified records so you can evaluate the data quality. No credit card, no commitment. Tell us your target specialty and geography.

How fast can I get data?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Free samples take 2-3 days.
