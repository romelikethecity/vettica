Vettica vs ZoomInfo for Veterinary Data

The short version

ZoomInfo is a general-purpose B2B contact database built for selling software to tech companies. It has 100M+ contacts, intent data, and workflow automation. It also costs $15K-$30K per year and has no veterinary-specific features.

Vettica is a purpose-built veterinary contact data service. We source from state licensing boards, segment by 30+ veterinary specialties, and verify every DVM's license status. Pricing is per-project with no annual contract.

If you're selling to veterinary practices specifically, Vettica delivers more accurate, more targeted data at a fraction of the cost. If you need contacts across dozens of industries, ZoomInfo is the broader tool.

Accuracy

ZoomInfo aggregates contact data from web scraping, email signatures, browser extensions, and data partnerships. This works well for tech companies with large online footprints. It works poorly for veterinary practices, which tend to be small businesses with limited digital presence.

Vettica sources from state veterinary licensing boards, the authoritative record of every practicing DVM. We cross-reference against AVMA directories and DEA registrations. The result is data that's verified against the same records that state regulators use.

Specialty segmentation

ZoomInfo classifies veterinary businesses under a handful of industry codes. There's no distinction between a small animal clinic, an equine hospital, and a pet grooming business. If you're selling equine surgical equipment, you can't filter for equine surgeons.

Vettica segments by 8 major categories and 30+ subspecialties. Small animal general practice, equine sports medicine, exotic avian, emergency critical care, and everything in between. This granularity only exists because we built our system specifically for veterinary data.

Pricing

ZoomInfo's pricing starts around $15,000/year for a single seat with limited credits. Enterprise plans run $30K-$60K+. You're paying for access to a platform with 100M contacts, most of which are irrelevant to your veterinary sales motion.

Vettica charges $0.50-$1.50 per record on a per-project basis. No annual contract. No seat licenses. A list of 1,000 verified DVMs in your target specialty costs $500-$1,500, delivered in 3-5 days. Your data to keep forever.

When to use ZoomInfo

If your company sells to multiple industries and needs a broad, multi-purpose database. If you need intent data and workflow automation. If you have the budget for an enterprise platform and the team to manage it.

When to use Vettica

If you sell specifically to veterinary practices. If you need license-verified DVM contacts segmented by specialty. If you want accurate data without an annual contract. If you'd rather spend your budget on data quality than platform access.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vettica a replacement for ZoomInfo or Apollo?

Not exactly. Vettica is a data service, not a platform. We provide the contact data. You use it in whatever sales tools you already have. Many teams use Vettica for veterinary contacts and keep their existing platform for workflow automation.

How does pricing compare?

Vettica charges $0.50-$1.50 per record, per project. No annual contract. A list of 1,000 DVMs costs $500-$1,500. ZoomInfo starts at $15K/year. Apollo starts at $99/month. Vettica is significantly cheaper for veterinary-specific data.

Can I import Vettica data into my CRM?

Yes. We deliver clean CSV files that import into any CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, etc.) or email platform (Outreach, Salesloft, Lemlist, etc.).
