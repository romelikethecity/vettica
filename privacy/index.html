Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information we collect

When you submit a form on getvettica.com, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, company name, phone number, and message content. We use this information to respond to your inquiry and deliver the data you requested.

We also collect standard web analytics data (page views, referral sources, device type) through Google Analytics 4 with Consent Mode v2. This data is anonymized and used to understand how visitors use our website.

How we use your information

We use your contact information to respond to inquiries, deliver data products, and follow up on orders. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your contact information with other companies for marketing purposes.

Cookies and tracking

We use first-party cookies for analytics (Google Analytics 4) and advertising measurement (Meta Pixel). Both are subject to our consent banner. If you decline cookies, no tracking data is collected beyond what is technically necessary for the website to function.

Data we sell

Vettica sells business contact data for veterinary practices. This data is sourced from publicly available records including state veterinary licensing boards, business registrations, and professional directories. We do not sell personal health information, patient data, or any data protected under HIPAA.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing us. If you are a veterinary professional whose contact information appears in our data products and you would like to be excluded from future lists, contact us and we will add you to our suppression file.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, email rome@getprovyx.com.
