Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a minimum order?

No minimum. You can order a list for a single ZIP code, a metro area, or an entire state. Pricing is per-project based on record count and data complexity.

What's included in each record?

DVM name, verified email, direct phone, practice name, full address, state license number, license status, and specialty type. Custom fields available on request.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes. Multi-state orders and recurring builds are priced more favorably. Contact us with your requirements and we'll put together a custom quote.

How do I get the free sample?

Fill out the contact form with your target criteria (specialty, geography). We'll build 20 verified records so you can evaluate the data quality before committing to a paid order.

Can I get a refund if the data is bad?

If deliverability falls below our stated rate on a verified list, we'll replace the affected records at no charge. We stand behind the accuracy of every list we build.
