Pricing FAQ

What's included in each record?

Every record includes DVM name, verified email, direct phone, practice name, full address, state license number, license status, and specialty type. Growth plans add technology detection and practice firmographics.

Can I get a sample before purchasing?

Yes. We provide 20 free verified records matched to your target criteria so you can evaluate data quality before committing to a purchase.

Do you offer email and mobile enrichment?

Email and direct phone are standard in every record. Mobile phone enrichment is available as an add-on for contacts where a mobile number is available.

How fast can I get my data?

Most orders ship within 3-5 business days. Free samples take 2-3 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive projects.

Is there a minimum order?

No minimum. You can order a list for a single ZIP code, a metro area, or an entire state. Pricing is per-project based on record count and complexity.

Do any services require annual contracts?

No. Contact data is pay-per-order. Ongoing builds are month-to-month. No annual commitments on any product.
